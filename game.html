Rules & Scoring

Complete rules for the WC2026 Prediction Championship.

⚽ How to Play

  • Step 1 — Before each match kicks off, enter your predicted score (e.g. 2–1) and save. Predictions submitted after kick-off are VOID.
  • Step 2 — The organiser (admin) enters the actual result after each match is played.
  • Step 3 — Points are calculated automatically and the leaderboard updates instantly.
  • Step 4 — The player with the most points at the end of the tournament wins!

🏟 Group Stage Scoring

Wrong prediction

Exact score → 5 pts: You predicted the precise final score (e.g. 2–1) and the match ended exactly 2–1. This is the highest reward in the group stage.

Correct result → 2 pts: You got the winner right (or predicted a draw that ended as a draw) but the exact score was wrong. Example: predicted 3–0, actual 1–0 — still a win, so 2 pts.

Wrong result → 0 pts: You predicted a win for one team and the other won, or predicted a draw but one team won.

🏆 Knockout Round Scoring

4

0
Wrong prediction

Exact score (90 min) → 10 pts: You predicted the correct score at 90 minutes. Extra time and penalties do NOT count for scoring — only the 90-minute result matters.

Correct result → 4 pts: Got the right winner (or draw after 90 min) but wrong exact score.

Wrong result → 0 pts: Prediction missed completely.

⚖️ Tiebreakers

  • Tiebreaker 1 — Most exact scores (5-pt or 10-pt predictions) wins the tie.
  • Tiebreaker 2 — If still tied, most correct result directions wins.
  • Tiebreaker 3 — If still level, the player who predicted the most group winners correctly wins. If all else fails — split the prize pool!

🎁 Bonus Predictions (optional)

  • Tournament winner → 20 pts — Before the tournament starts, each player picks the overall champion. Lock picks in before June 11.
  • Top scorer → 10 pts — Pick the player who finishes with the most goals. Ties in actual top scorers = all correct pickers earn the points.
  • Golden Glove → 10 pts — Pick the best goalkeeper of the tournament (official FIFA award).
  • Biggest upset → 5 pts — Pick one group-stage match where a lower-seeded team beats a higher-seeded team. Must be locked in before the tournament.

🎖 Player Badges

  • 🏆 Legend — 20+ exact scores
  • 🎯 Sniper — 12+ exact scores
  • 💯 Century — 100+ total points
  • 🔥 Hot Streak — 6+ exact scores
  • ⭐ Rising Star — 50+ total points
  • ✨ Sharp Eye — 3+ exact scores
  • 🌱 First Blood — 1+ exact score
  • 🤞 Rookie — still waiting for that first exact!

📋 Fair Play Rules

  • Deadline — All predictions must be submitted before the match kicks off. The organiser must not accept late entries under any circumstances.
  • Organiser neutrality — The organiser enters actual scores only — they cannot change player predictions after kick-off.
  • Disputed results — In case of a disputed score entry, the official FIFA match report is the authoritative source.
  • Fun first! — This game is meant to be fun. If played for money, agree on the prize pool before the tournament begins. Play fair, enjoy the football!
